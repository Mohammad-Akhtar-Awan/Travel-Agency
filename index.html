Services

We book air tickets and hotels, assist in visa applications and manage religious tours to Saudi Arabia, Iraq, Iran and Syria.

Ticket and Hotel Booking

Your can order us to book air ticket of any airline for any destination. Just let's know your destination and we will book ticket immedieately after receiving payment. You don't need to worry about reserving your luxury hotel. We will book a hotel and get ready a residence in advance.

Visa Cosultancy

We provide visa and immigration consultancy. If you are planning to move abroad, we assist you in getting visa with convenience. Just send us your documents to proceed for your visa application.

Tours Management

We are expert in managing domestic and international tours to multiple locations all across the world. In addition to exploring natural beauty of Pakistan, our popular destinations are in Europe, Canada, US, Australia, South Africa and Middle East. Furthermore, we arrange religious and spirtual tours to Saudi Arabia, Iraq, Iran and Syria. See Tour Packages
